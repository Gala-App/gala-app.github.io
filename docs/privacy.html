Last Updated: December 20, 2025

Gala LLC ("Gala," "we," "us," or "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use the Gala mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using Gala, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

Legal Entity: Gala LLC

Contact Email: admin@galatheapp.com

2. Users Covered by This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Gala, including:

  • Clients / Customers hiring bartenders, food vendors, music artists and entertainment
  • Service Providers ("Talents") offering bartending services, music, food and entertainment
  • Internal Administrative Staff managing the platform

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo
  • Account preferences and settings
  • In-app messages and communications

3.2 Authentication & Verification

Gala uses Google and Apple Sign-In as the sole login methods. We do not collect or store passwords. Authentication data is provided by Google and Apple to verify your identity and create your account.

3.3 Background Checks

For service providers, identity verification and background screening may be conducted through third-party services connected to Google and Apple authentication. Gala does not store sensitive background check documents directly.

3.4 Location Information

  • Clients: Precise location (to enable service matching)
  • Service Providers: Approximate location only (city/area level)

Location data is used solely to facilitate service availability and matching.

4. Payments & Financial Information

All payments and payouts are processed securely through Stripe. Gala does not store full credit card or bank account numbers. Stripe handles all sensitive financial data in accordance with industry security standards.

5. Communications

  • In-app messaging
  • Push notifications
  • SMS text messages related to service activity and account updates

6. Device & Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

This information helps us improve app performance and reliability.

7. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Authenticate users
  • Match clients with service providers
  • Facilitate bookings, payments, and payouts
  • Enable in-app communication
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve platform performance and security
  • Prevent fraud and misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

8. Sharing of Information

We may share personal information:

  • Between clients and service providers (e.g., name, profile, service details)
  • With third-party service providers, including:
    • Stripe (payments)

We do not sell personal information.

Gala relies on trusted third parties to operate, including:

These providers process data only as necessary to perform services on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect your information.
